Core Concepts

What the simulator computes

01

State Vector

The simulator stores every basis amplitude as a complex number and updates all affected amplitudes when gates are applied.

02

Superposition

Hadamard gates distribute amplitude across basis states, turning one classical input into a weighted quantum state.

03

Entanglement

Controlled gates couple amplitudes across qubits, producing correlations that cannot be described independently.

04

Measurement

Shot counts are sampled from each basis state's probability, which is the squared magnitude of its amplitude.
